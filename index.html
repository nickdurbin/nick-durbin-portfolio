Intro

Thank you for looking over my portfolio site. I am software developer through the Lambda School program with emphasis on the MERN stack, but also knowledge of Java, Ruby/Rails, C, and both MongoDB and SQL databases. You can see some of my work here.

Before pursuing web development I worked as a Project Manager for a startup company called PokerSports, which specialized in a unique take on traditional fantasy sports. Also, I was a manager of Test and Repair, as well as, our RMA Department at an automation company called Applied Vision Corp. You can find my LOR's and list of responsibilities on my resume and Linked-In.
